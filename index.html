Welcome to Jane's World

Jane is a passionate case manager for Child Protective Services, dedicated to supporting families and advocating for LGBTQ+ initiatives. A proud organizer of Denver Pride’s workplace booth, Jane is committed to uplifting minority communities. Outside of work, they thrive on creativity—hiking with their dog Juno, cosplaying, gaming, and making art. A natural extrovert, Jane loves connecting with others through local events and murder mystery parties. With dreams of traveling the world and writing a novel, they continue to balance advocacy, adventure, and storytelling in their journey.
